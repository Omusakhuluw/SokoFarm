We will Rejoice

By Francis Wekesa

When the time has come for us to kiss our temporary homes goodbye,
We will leave quietly and joyfully to our six feet low,
Our loved ones will mourn for us feeling lonely and sorry for us,
But they don't know that its Jesus who took us to Hiw wonderful place.

We will sing and sing with joy for the Wonderful Most High God,
We will dance and dance with joy for the Most Marvelors God,
He will welcome us to His abode and say my child relax,
For you've accomplished everything that you chose to learn on Earth.
